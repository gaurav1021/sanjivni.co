Dr. Sanjiv Kapoor

CONSULTANT RHEUMATOLOGIST M.D. (Medicine) , D.M. (Clinical Immunology)

Dr Sanjiv Kapoor is a Senior Consultant Rheumatologist in Indian Spinal Injury Center, Vasant Kunj and Sanjivni Rheumatology and infertility centre, Janakpuri. He has an extensive experience in latest medications including Biologics and diagnostic tools used in Rheumatoid Arthritis,Ankylosing Spondylitis, lower backache, SLE (Lupus), Sjogrens,Scleroderma and other rheumatological diseases and their complications.
