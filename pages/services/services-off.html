Services Offered



Facilities/Services available



Comprehensive diagnosis and evaluation using latest state of art technology like –

  Evaluation and Care for All Types of Arthritis and rheumatological diseases

  Diseases assessment as per international guidelines (DAS-28/SLEDAI/BASDAI etc)

  Musculoskeletal diagnosed ultrasound

  Nail fold capillaroscopy

  Schirmer test

  Salivary test

  Salivary gland, muscle and skin biopsy

  Crystal exam in microscopy for gout and pseudogout

  Intra-articular joint injection and aspiration (arthocentesis)

  Soft tissue injection – Tendon Sheath Steroid Injections, Carpal Tunnel Injections

  Ultrasound guided injection for deep and difficult joints

  Infusion Therapy for inflammatory arthritis and osteoporosis

  Advanced Biological Therapy for Connective Tissue Disorders (i.e., Reditux, Remicade, Enbrel, Humira, etc.)

  Lumbar Facet Joint Injection

  Physiotherapy and occupational therapy

  Viscosupplementation- the injection of Artificial Joint Fluid
